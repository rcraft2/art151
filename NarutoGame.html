Naruto Run

I produced this small game when I made my Naruto fan page for an assignment. I enjoy playing games, and have come to enjoy making them as well because I love making my work interactive. To play the game just use your mouse to click to jump over the incoming shurikens. Each jumped shuriken is worth 4 points, and your highscore for the session is saved.
